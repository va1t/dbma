 About

DBM Associates has been providing technology solutions to companies in New Jersey, and bordering areas of New York and Pennsylvania since 1985. Our consultants and engineers each have more than 20 years of experience in managing and implementing information technology for companies ranging from small single sites to multi-national enterprises. Clients come to DBM Associates because we can help them to get the maximum benefit from their technology investments.

Our business is built on being the outsourced "IT Department" for small businesses. Providing everything you would expect from an in-house staff: technology planning; procurement and implementation; user training and support; and day-to-day monitoring and administration.

Contact us today to set up an appointment to discuss your requirements and concerns.

 Directions to DBM Associates

DBM Associates is located in One Salem Square at 295 US Route 22 East, Lebanon, NJ. (mailing address is Whitehouse Station, NJ.)

We are on Route 22, approximately 1.5 miles West of County Route 523.

GPS: Use the 295 US Route 22 East, Lebanon, NJ address. The same address in Whitehouse Station is nowhere close.

From New York and Northern New Jersey

Take Route 78 West to exit 24 (Route 523 / Oldwick exit). Take 523 South to US Route 22 West.

From Central New Jersey

Take US Route 22 West to Whitehouse Station. Continue on through double traffic lights for County Route 523.

From Trenton and Philadelphia

Take 95 North to Route 31 North. Stay on 31 North (almost forever) through Flemington. After passing through the business section of Flemington, watch carefully for the sign (at a traffic light) for Whitehouse Station, 523. Take 523 North (turn right) to US Route 22 West. If you pass Flemington Raceway you've passed 523, so make a right on to 620 North at the Getty gas station, and join up with 523 North after a few miles.

Final Approach (from intersection of 523 and 22)

DBM is located in the One Salem Square building, which is about 1.5 miles West of County Route 523 North on the Eastbound side of US Route 22. After passing the light for 523 North (at the Exxon gas station), you go West through one more traffic light, then make the second U-Turn through the median (you will see Haver's Lawnmower and Garden Center on the left just before the U-Turn). One Salem Square is the first driveway after the U-Turn (also the first driveway after Mountain Road if you are coming up 22 East from Clinton or points Westward). The DBM Offices are located in the rear right-side of the building.
